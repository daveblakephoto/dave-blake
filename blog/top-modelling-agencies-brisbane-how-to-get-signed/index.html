Top Modelling Agencies in Brisbane + How to Get Signed

By Dave Blake Published July 21, 2025 Updated August 4, 2025

Brisbane has a strong modelling ecosystem. If you are aiming to get signed, your first lever is a clean, natural, agency-ready portfolio. This guide highlights top agencies and practical submission advice.

Why A Strong Portfolio Matters

Agencies do not need over-produced glamour shots. They want clear images that show your face, body proportions, movement, and versatility. A focused folio signals that you are ready for test shoots, castings, campaigns, and runway consideration.

Top Modelling Agencies In Brisbane

  1. Vivien's Model Management

    One of Australia's most established agencies with strong development pathways from local representation to international placements.

    Website: viviensmodels.com.au/brisbane · Known for editorial, high fashion, and commercial talent.

  2. Chic Brisbane

    A major network with strong editorial and runway focus, connected to national and international opportunities.

    Website: chicbrisbane.com.au · Known for scouting fresh faces and editorial bookings.

Tips For Agency Submissions

  • Follow each agency's submission format exactly.
  • Use natural light and minimal retouching for digitals.
  • Include clean headshots and full-body frames.
  • Prioritize authentic expression over heavy styling.
  • Work with a photographer experienced in agency standards.

How To Get Agency-Ready

A portfolio session should cover clean headshots, full-body frames, editorial looks, and beauty options. The goal is a tight book that reads immediately in a castings workflow.
