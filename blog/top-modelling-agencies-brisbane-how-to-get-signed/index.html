Top Modelling Agencies in Brisbane + How to Get Signed

July 21, 2025 in Model Resources, Industry Guides

Brisbane is home to a thriving modelling industry, and if you're dreaming of getting signed, you're in the right place. Whether you're just starting out or levelling up your experience, choosing the right agency is key. But before you hit "submit" on any application, there’s one essential element you can’t skip: a professional, agency-ready model portfolio.

I've helped hundreds of models get signed with clean, editorial-style folio images. Let's dive into the top agencies in Brisbane and how you can stand out.

Why a Strong Portfolio Matters

Every agency below wants to see what you look like in front of a camera, but not just any selfies. They want clean, natural images that showcase your range, angles and personality. A great model folio shows that you're ready for test shoots, campaigns, and runway castings.

Top Modelling Agencies in Brisbane

1. Vivien's Model Management

Vivien’s is one of Australia’s most established and respected agencies, with a legacy dating back to 1967. Their Brisbane division represents a curated roster of talent ranging from fresh faces to working professionals, and they have a strong reputation for developing models with both commercial and international runway potential. Vivien’s takes a holistic and hands-on approach to career development, nurturing everything from test shoots and comp cards to global placements with agencies in New York, Milan, and London. If you’re looking for an agency with real industry pull and a serious eye for timeless beauty, Vivien’s is a name to know.

  • Website: viviensmodels.com.au/brisbane

  • Known for: Editorial, high fashion, and commercial talent

2. Chic Brisbane

While headquartered in Sydney, Chic Management maintains a strong presence in Brisbane, thanks to its roots in the former Dallys agency. Known for launching the careers of international stars, Chic places a strong emphasis on editorial, high fashion, and runway work. Their Brisbane team scouts talent locally, offering models access to national and global opportunities through one of Australia’s most respected networks.

  • Website: chicbrisbane.com.au

  • Known for: Scouting new faces, editorial bookings

Top Modelling Agencies Near Brisbane (Gold Coast)

1. Saint Model Management

Founded by Mandy St George, Saint Model Management is a boutique Gold Coast agency that has quickly made a name for itself with its clean aesthetic and selective roster. Known for discovering fresh talent and nurturing them into agency-ready professionals, Saint maintains strong connections with both local and interstate brands. They regularly book for eCommerce, fashion week, and editorial-style campaigns, offering a stepping stone for models with big aspirations.

  • Website: saintmodel.com

  • Known for: High-end development, fashion castings, emerging model careers

Emerging Modelling Agencies in Brisbane

1. LOSTANDFOUND Management

LOSTANDFOUND is a Brisbane-based boutique mother agency with a focus on early-stage development and existing model re-branding with a fashion-forward edge. Led by industry professionals with international experience, they bridge the gap between local talent and major markets like Sydney, Paris, and New York. LOSTANDFOUND is known for curating authentic, expressive portfolios and forging close mentorship-style relationships with their models.

  • Website: lostandfound-mgmt.com

  • Known for: Model development, expressive portfolios, international placement

2. Ghost Management

Founded by model Alex Ford, Ghost Management is a creative-led modelling and talent agency rooted in Brisbane. Known for championing individuality, they cast across fashion, commercial, and editorial spaces. Ghost blends traditional agency work with a more inclusive and artistic approach to representation, often working with talent who challenge norms and bring something unique to the table.

  • Website: ghostmanagement.com.au

  • Known for: Inclusive casting, creative campaigns, emerging talent

Tips for Approaching Agencies

  • Follow their submission instructions: Most will ask for digitals or polaroids (natural light, minimal makeup)

  • Don't over-edit your photos: Agencies want to see the real you

  • Include a mix of headshots and full body shots

  • Be professional: First impressions matter

  • Get agency-ready images: Avoid ‘glamour’ photography studios

  • Work with an industry photographer: It doesn’t have to be me, but choose someone who regularly shoots signed models and real fashion campaigns.

How to Get Agency-Ready with Your Portfolio

Getting signed starts with the right images. My model folio sessions are designed to give you everything an agency wants to see:

  • Clean headshots

  • Natural full-body shots

  • Editorial fashion looks

  • Beauty images

Final Thoughts

Brisbane has a solid network of model agencies, each with its own strengths. But the first step to getting signed is making sure your portfolio reflects your potential. If you’re serious about modelling, take the leap and get professional images that put you on the radar.
