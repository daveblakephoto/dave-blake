Chloe Wheatland – Ferrari Editorial Outtakes

Chloe Wheatland sits behind the wheel of a vintage Ferrari 308 GTS on Philip Island, captured in warm Ektachrome tones. The image is a quiet nod to 80s film nostalgia, with tinted sunglasses, soft grain, and retro rebellion in a red blur of metal and attitude.
