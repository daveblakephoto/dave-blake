DaveBlake_NYFW-OliviaVinten-001.jpg

A powerful portrait of the world-renowned Danish model, Olivia Vinten, captured post her walk at the Zimmerman show during New York Fashion Week. Her thick, lioness-like blonde hair dominates the frame, emphasizing her regal presence. With a gaze directed away from the camera, she exudes a sense of poise and contemporary elegance.

DaveBlake_StudioBW-AnishaProfile-001.jpg

A soulful black and white studio portrait of Anisha Okpalanze, a distinguished black model. The soft, grainy texture of the image accentuates her profile, with her mouth slightly open in a silent expression. Her raised arms create a unique silhouette, with her triceps covering her ears and forearms falling gracefully behind her head. A tattoo of a love heart with an upside-down face adds a touch of intrigue to the composition.

DaveBlake_BeautyStory-RosesAndRevelations-001_159.jpg

"Roses and Revelations" is a captivating beauty story that unfolds the transformative journey of a young woman, portrayed by the talented model Alicia Herbeth. The story, captured by photographer Dave Blake, explores the essence of change and discovery through the symbolism of the red rosea classic emblem of passion and beauty. As the narrative progresses, we witness the model's evolution from pure, fresh-faced innocence to a more intense and powerful allure, with the red rose serving as the catalyst for her transformation. Each frame peels back layers of her persona, much like the delicate yet striking layers of a rose, revealing a multifaceted depth. Vulnerability and empowerment intertwine as the story's petals unfold, leading to a profound embrace of the model's unique beauty and inner strength.

DaveBlake_EktachromeShoot-BronteSun-001-1.jpg

Byron Bay - 2020"An evocative photo capturing Bronte, her tanned skin accentuated by the 80s Ektachrome film grading. With eyes closed, she basks in the sun's warmth, her emotion palpable. Her hair, adorned with small plaits secured by bright blue elastic bands, complements the dark saturated blue sky. Delicate shell necklaces drape her neck, and her dewy lips reflect the sun's glow.

DaveBlake_BeautyShoot-LongLensTribute-001.jpg

A vivid and tightly cropped photograph showcasing lips sipping from an orange straw, set against a soft green/cyan background. The use of a 300mm lens beautifully blurs the backdrop, emphasizing the contrast between the pink lips and the straw. This composition is a nod to the legendary photographer Hans Feurer, renowned for his unique approach to capturing beauty with long lenses.

DaveBlake_BB-TestShoot-80sShorts-001.jpg

A close-up shot capturing the intricate details of breathable white cotton shorts, reminiscent of the 80s Ektachrome style. The tanned model, positioned near Byron Bay, Australia, draws attention to the shorts with a gunslinger hand gesture. The frame focuses from just above her knees to just under her sternum, emphasizing the shorts' design.

DaveBlake_GrittyPrettyShoot-BackDetail-001.jpg

A black and white photograph, featured in GrittyPretty Magazine, capturing the delicate allure of a model's back. The fine silver chain that drapes partway down her back adds a touch of elegance. The sun on her skin creates a radiant glow, emphasizing the soft and feminine definition, and showcasing every intricate detail.

Lissy's Ballet Elegance: Double-Exposure in Monochrome

A masterful black and white fine art photograph captures Lissy, a ballet dancer, in the studio. Set against a profound black backdrop, she stands naked, her form accentuated by a captivating skirt of light. Her hair, styled in a neat bun, complements the ethereal grace of her elegant arching body, which fills the frame intriguingly. The shot, taken through a prism, introduces a double-exposure effect, adding layers of depth and mystique to the composition.

DaveBlake_GC-TestShoot-MichelleB-001.jpg

A striking black and white photograph of Michelle Bagarra, gracefully arching her body in a yoga bridge pose on the beach. Dressed in a sleek black swimsuit, the shot captures her strength and poise against the serene backdrop of the Gold Coast, Australia.

DaveBlake_StudioShoot-BelleVive-001.jpg

A warm black and white studio photograph of Belle, captured from the back, focusing from her shoulder blades upwards. Her hair, styled in a simple yet undone loose plait, adds a touch of casual elegance. The tattoo on the back of her neck, reading "vive", serves as a poignant focal point, symbolizing life and vivacity.

DaveBlake_DenimShoot-JaseMotorcycle-001.jpg

A compelling photograph of Jase Brown for a denim campaign, capturing him seated on a motorcycle, shirtless, exuding a blend of femininity and masculinity. A tattoo adorns one arm, while his shirt rests in his lap. His long hair, tied in a loose ponytail, gracefully whisps in the wind, adding movement and dynamism to the shot.

DaveBlake_FieldShoot-KateHorse-001.jpg

In a serene field, Kate stands mid-movement, her blonde hair wisping across her face. She dons a black dress that exudes both elegance and a modern country aesthetic, reminiscent of ready-to-wear collections that empower contemporary women. Behind her, a horse with pricked ears complements her stance. Both Kate and the horse gaze into the camera, creating a unified and powerful presence.
